About

Aroma Stretch is a combination of Stretch Exercise with Aromatherapy. It is the ideal balance of body and mind, improving health and enhancing body movements. This class uses Essential Senses to aid in stress and mood management.

About the trainer

4 years experience

Specialises in Yoga Stretch, Pilates 101, Pilates 201

Teaches at Ang Mo Kio CC, Toa Payoh CC

Jacinta Wee

Jacinta Wee has been conducting wellness related programs with People’s Association since 1993. A fitness trainer with a degree in Psychology, and having worked with groups of varying ages and levels of fitness, there is no lack of creativity in Jacinta programs. Jacinta aimed to develop programs that enhance participants› workout experience through brain-stimulating and pro-active learning.

Things to note

  1. Pre-requisite: No pre-requisite
  2. Requirements: All students are required to bring along their own yoga mat for this class.
  3. Cancellation policy: Once class starts, no cancellation or refund will be made.
